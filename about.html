Our story

Dallas's Reliable Towing Company

To provide fast, affordable, and professional towing and roadside assistance to the Dallas community, 24/7.

Terry Towing was founded with a simple mission: help stranded drivers get back on the road quickly and safely. Over the years, we've grown from a single truck to a fleet serving all of Dallas. Our team shares a commitment to customer service and reliability.

We understand that breakdowns and accidents are stressful. That's why we focus on prompt response, clear communication, and fair pricing. Whether it's a simple jump-start or a complex heavy-duty tow, we treat every customer with respect and professionalism.
